The Gap · 2008 — 2020
Twelve Years in the Field

Between Sheridan and Colorado Mountain College, I spent twelve years building real skills in real environments — kitchens, trails, theaters, event floors, and eventually a diner of my own. I managed people, money, inventory, crises, and competing priorities without a degree to back me up. When COVID closed A&K's Place on opening day in 2020, it became clear that the instincts were there — they just needed the framework. That's what sent me back to school. Everything in this portfolio came from that decision.
